2. Organization Information
 
About Us
 
Sportera Global is a sports development and event management platform dedicated to creating opportunities for athletes through competitions, leagues, talent identification programs, and international exposure initiatives. The organization aims to bridge the gap between grassroots participation and professional sporting opportunities.
 
Vision
 
To become a globally recognized platform that empowers athletes through sports development, education, competition, and international opportunities.
 
Mission
 
To provide structured pathways for athletes by organizing high-quality sporting events, leagues, development programs, and international exposure opportunities.
