Sportera Global

About the Program

Sportera Global organizes international football exposure tours for players, teams, coaches, academies, and clubs. These tours provide opportunities to compete internationally, experience different football cultures, and gain valuable exposure on the world stage.

Our international exposure program bridges the gap between local talent and global opportunities — taking Indian football to the world and bringing the world's football culture back home.

Program Overview

What our international exposure tours offer

International Competition

Compete against teams from across the world in structured tournament formats.

Cultural Exchange

Experience different football cultures and build lasting international connections.

Coaching Exposure

Learn from international coaches and observe elite training methodologies.

Tournament Experience

Participate in international tournaments and gain competitive global experience.

Player Development

Structured development pathways with exposure to international playing standards.

Full Tour Management

End-to-end management including travel, accommodation, meals, and match logistics.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Players, teams, coaches, academies & clubs
  • All age categories (U9 to Senior)
  • Valid passport required
  • Parent/guardian consent for players under 18
  • Medical fitness clearance

Tour Objectives

  • International competitive exposure
  • Talent identification on a global platform
  • Technical & tactical development
  • Building international sports networks
  • Cultural learning & personal growth
  • Representing India on the world stage
