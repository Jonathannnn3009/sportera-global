About IF5L

The Indian Football 5 League (IF5L) is India's premier Football 5 league, created to provide a structured, competitive, and professional platform for players, clubs, academies, schools, and teams across the country.

Designed to promote grassroots football and player development, IF5L features city, state, and national-level competitions across multiple age categories. The league combines professional tournament management with modern technology, including online registrations, digital player IDs, live fixtures, results, and standings.

Beyond competition, IF5L aims to create pathways for talent identification, national recognition, and international exposure, helping players take the next step in their football journey. Our vision is to build India's largest Football 5 league and make quality football competition accessible in every city and state.
