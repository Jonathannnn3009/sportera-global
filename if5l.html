About IF5L

The Indian Football 5 League (IF5L) is a structured youth football league platform operating under Football 5 Association. It provides a professional competitive environment for teams across India.

The league follows a format consisting of a League Stage followed by Knockout rounds, ensuring every team gets substantial match time while building toward an exciting climax.

IF5L features franchise teams from major cities competing for the coveted championship title, with comprehensive statistics, awards, and player recognition programs.

Tournament Format

How the Indian Football 5 League works

League Stage

All 8 teams play each other in a round-robin format. Points awarded for wins and draws determine standings.

Semi-Finals

Top 4 teams from the league stage qualify for the semi-finals. 1st vs 4th and 2nd vs 3rd in knockout format.

Grand Final

Semi-final winners compete in the Grand Final to be crowned IF5L Champions. Third-place playoff also held.
