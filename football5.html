Our Story

Football 5 Association is a dedicated football development division under Sportera Global, responsible for grassroots football development, national championships, technical programs, and football community growth across India.

Football 5 is a fast-paced, exciting format of football played with 5 players on each side on a smaller pitch with boards on the sides. There is no offside rule, and each half lasts 12-15 minutes, making it an action-packed experience for players and spectators alike.

Our mission is to develop football at every level — from community football to national championships — providing athletes with structured pathways to grow and succeed.

The Game

Key rules that make Football 5 unique and exciting

5v5 Format

Five players per side including the goalkeeper, creating fast and dynamic gameplay.

Smaller Pitch

Players are involved in the game more often, leading to faster skill development. Limited space forces players to think and react quickly.

12-15 Minute Halves

Two halves of 12-15 minutes each, keeping the intensity high throughout the match.

Fast-Paced

With more ball touches, quicker decision-making, and continuous action, players develop their technical skills, fitness, teamwork, and confidence faster.
